Powerful Features for Peaceful Coexistence

Our platform combines technology with traditional wisdom to create lasting solutions

Smart Land Mapping

Farmers can mark and highlight important farmland areas. All users can see marked territories in real-time, preventing accidental grazing on crops.

  • GPS-accurate boundaries
  • Crop type indicators
  • Harvest timeline alerts

Grazing Route Planning

Herders can mark safe grazing routes and schedules. Share movement plans with the community to avoid conflicts and find the best pastures.

  • Seasonal route optimization
  • Water point locations
  • Community notifications

Direct Communication

When conflicts arise, users can message each other directly. Resolve issues through dialogue before they escalate to violence.

  • Instant messaging system
  • Conflict resolution tools
  • Community mediators
